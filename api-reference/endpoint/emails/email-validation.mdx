## Use Case:
Clean and verify email lists to improve campaign success rates and lower bounce rates.

## Pricing
**$0.005** per validation at Tier 0 (base price).

Each validation request checks the deliverability of a single email address, including catch-all domain detection. You are charged per validation performed.

<Tip>Your price per validation decreases automatically as your lifetime spend grows. [Check your current tier and pricing →](https://beta.generect.com/settings/billing)</Tip>
