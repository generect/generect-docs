## Use Case:
Retrieve a fresh lead profile, including reverse email lookup, by Generect ID, LinkedIn URL, or email.

## Pricing
This is a billable endpoint — you are charged only when a lead is found and returned. If nothing is found, the request is free.

<Tip>Your price decreases automatically as your lifetime spend grows. [Check your current tier and pricing →](https://beta.generect.com/settings/billing)</Tip>
