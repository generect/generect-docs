## Use Case:
Show masked lead results in SaaS products without exposing paid data such as email, phone, LinkedIn URL, or company domain.

## Pricing
**$0.002** per result at Tier 0 (base price).

Preview returns masked lead data without exposing paid fields (email, phone, LinkedIn URL, domain). This is the most cost-effective way to browse and filter leads before committing to full enrichment.
