## Use Case:
Show masked lead results in SaaS products without exposing paid data such as email, phone, LinkedIn URL, or company domain.

## Pricing
This endpoint consumes credits per returned result. If no leads are found, no credit is charged. See [Pricing](/billing/pay-as-you-go) for credit costs.
