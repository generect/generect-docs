Generect uses a transparent Pay-as-you-go model — you only pay for what you actually use.

No subscriptions, no hidden fees — just real consumption.

## Core principles of the model

1. **You pay only for results.**  
   For example, Email Finder charges only for a *valid* found email, and UI Search is always free to use.
2. **No monthly plans.**  
   You're not locked into subscriptions — use the platform at your own pace.
3. **Pricing decreases as you grow.**  
   The higher your lifetime spend, the better your pricing tier becomes, and the cheaper each future action costs.
4. **A positive balance is required.**  
   Email Finder, API usage, exporting data, and email validation all require a positive balance.  
   If your balance reaches zero, you won't be able to use these features until you top up.

## Typical Charges

Below is a quick overview of the actions that commonly generate costs.  
Exact pricing depends on your tier.

| Action | What You Pay For | Price Range |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Email Finder (valid email)** | Finding a verified corporate email | **\$0.030 → \$0.012** depending on tier |
| **Exporting contacts** | Exporting leads from Search | **\$0.020 → \$0.018** |
| **API request (live search)** | Real-time data retrieval | **\$0.020 → \$0.005** |
| **API cached result** | Returning previously generated data | **\$0.0067 → \$0.0017** |
| **Phone Finder (valid number)** | Finding a verified direct phone | **0.4 credits per valid number** (fixed) |
| **Email Verification** | Validating an existing email | **\$0.005** |
| **UI Search** | Browsing + filtering | **Free — you only pay for discovered emails** |

<Tip>

</Tip>
