Generect uses a transparent Pay-as-you-go model — you only pay for what you actually use.

No subscriptions, no hidden fees — just real consumption.

## Core principles of the model

1. **You pay only for results.**  
   For example, Email Finder charges only for a *valid* found email, and enrichment charges only when data is found.
2. **No monthly plans.**  
   You're not locked into subscriptions — use the platform at your own pace.
3. **Pricing decreases as you grow.**  
   The higher your lifetime spend, the better your pricing tier becomes, and the cheaper each future action costs.
4. **A positive balance is required.**  
   Email Finder, API usage, exporting data, and email validation all require a positive balance.  
   If your balance reaches zero, you won't be able to use these features until you top up.

## Typical Charges

Across the API the rule is simple: **you are charged 1 credit per billable result** — a search result, a valid email, a found phone, or an enriched record. If nothing is found, nothing is charged. Count, status, webhook, and account endpoints are free; real-time count requests cost 1 credit each.

| API action | What you pay for | Credits charged |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Preview leads** `POST /preview/leads/` | Masked lead preview result | **Free** |
| **Preview lead count** `POST /preview/leads/count/` | Counting matching preview leads | **Free** |
| **Database lead search** `POST /search/database/leads/` | Cached lead result | **1 credit per result** |
| **Database company search** `POST /search/database/companies/` | Cached company result | **1 credit per result** |
| **Database company-lead search** `POST /search/database/company-leads/` | Cached company and lead match | **1 credit per result** |
| **Database lead count** `POST /search/database/leads/count/` | Counting cached lead matches | **Free** |
| **Database company count** `POST /search/database/companies/count/` | Counting cached company matches | **Free** |
| **Database company-lead count** `POST /search/database/company-leads/count/` | Counting cached company-lead matches | **Free** |
| **Realtime lead search** `POST /search/realtime/leads/` | Fresh lead result | **1 credit per result** |
| **Realtime company search** `POST /search/realtime/companies/` | Fresh company result | **1 credit per result** |
| **Realtime company-lead search** `POST /search/realtime/company-leads/` | Fresh company and lead match | **1 credit per result** |
| **Realtime lead count** `POST /search/realtime/leads/count/` | Counting fresh lead matches | **1 credit per request** |
| **Realtime company count** `POST /search/realtime/companies/count/` | Counting fresh company matches | **1 credit per request** |
| **Database lead enrichment** `POST /enrich/database/lead/` | Full cached lead profile returned | **1 credit per result** (only when a lead is found) |
| **Realtime lead enrichment** `POST /enrich/realtime/lead/` | Full fresh lead profile returned | **1 credit per result** (only when a lead is found) |
| **Database company enrichment** `POST /enrich/database/company/` | Full cached company profile returned | **1 credit per result** (only when a company is found) |
| **Realtime company enrichment** `POST /enrich/realtime/company/` | Full fresh company profile returned | **1 credit per result** (only when a company is found) |
| **Email Finder** `POST /email/find/` | Valid email found by `lead_id`, `linkedin_url`, or name + domain | **1 credit per valid email** |
| **Bulk Email Finder** `POST /email/find/bulk/` | Valid email found in a bulk job | **1 credit per valid email** |
| **Bulk Email Finder status** `GET /email/find/bulk/{job_id}/` | Checking bulk email job status | **Free** |
| **Email Validation** `POST /email/validate/` | Email validation | **1 credit per email** |
| **Phone Finder** `POST /phone/find/` | Phone number found | **1 credit per found phone** |
| **Bulk Phone Finder** `POST /phone/find/bulk/` | Phone number found in a bulk job | **1 credit per found phone** |
| **Bulk Phone Finder status** `GET /phone/find/bulk/{job_id}/` | Checking bulk phone job status | **Free** |
| **List webhooks** `GET /webhooks/` | Registered webhook lookup | **Free** |
| **Register webhook** `POST /webhooks/` | Webhook registration | **Free** |
| **Retrieve webhook** `GET /webhooks/{id}/` | Single webhook lookup | **Free** |
| **Update webhook** `PUT` / `PATCH /webhooks/{id}/` | Webhook update | **Free** |
| **Delete webhook** `DELETE /webhooks/{id}/` | Webhook removal | **Free** |
| **Test webhook** `POST /webhooks/{id}/test/` | Test webhook delivery | **Free** |
| **Account profile** `GET /accounts/me/` | Profile, credit balance, and Preview tier lookup | **Free** |
| **Account usage** `GET /accounts/usage/` | Credit usage breakdown | **Free** |
| **Account transactions** `GET /accounts/transactions/` | Transaction history lookup | **Free** |

<Note>
  No Data, No Charge: if an email, phone, lead, or company is not found, Generect charges **0 credits** for that result.
</Note>

<Tip>
  The more you use Generect, the better your pricing becomes. Check your current tier in the Billing section to see your discounts.
</Tip>
