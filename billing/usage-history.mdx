The **Usage** page is your transparent financial activity log.  
Here you can see every transaction related to your balance in a clear, structured format.

## What you'll find in Usage

- **full transaction history** – balance top-ups, charges for Email Finder, API requests, exports, and other actions
- **date & exact time** of each operation
- **type of charge** (which tool or feature triggered the cost)
- **amount** for each transaction
- **time-period filters** – quickly view charges for:
  - past 1 day
  - past 7 days
  - past 30 days
  - a custom date range

The history updates automatically after every operation, so you always see real-time data with no delay.

This is your complete financial log – it helps you control spending, track price changes, and analyze your team's activity.
