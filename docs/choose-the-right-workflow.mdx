# I want to...
## Build a target account list
  Use ICP Search to define your ideal customer profile by industry, company size, location, revenue, technology stack, and more. Generect returns a list of matching companies in real time.
  - https://docs.generect.com/docs/icp-search
## Find decision-makers
  Search for specific contacts by role, title, seniority, or department within target companies. Get verified emails and direct phone numbers for the people who matter.
  - https://docs.generect.com/use-cases/saas/find-contacts
## Get verified emails
  Use the Email Finder to generate and validate corporate email addresses from a first name, last name, and company domain. Every email is automatically validated — no extra step required.
  - https://docs.generect.com/docs/email-finder 
## Verify a campaign list
  Upload your existing list and use bulk email validation to check deliverability before sending. Remove invalid, disposable, and catch-all addresses to protect your sender reputation.
  - https://docs.generect.com/docs/email-validation
  - https://docs.generect.com/use-cases/api/bulk-processing
## Enrich my CRM
  Push verified contact and company data directly into your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive). Keep records accurate and up to date without manual data entry.
  - https://docs.generect.com/use-cases/api/data-enrichment
  - https://docs.generect.com/use-cases/api/automations-and-integrations
