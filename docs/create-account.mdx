Follow these steps to create your Generect account and start discovering leads.

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. Go to [https://generect.com](https://generect.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top-right corner
3. Register using Google, Microsoft, or your work email  
  <Tip>
    Personal email domains like gmail.com or outlook.com are not recommended
  </Tip>
4. If you choose to sign up with your work email, create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
5. After logging in, you automatically receive $5 in free credits to test the platform
