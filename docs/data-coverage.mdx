Generect covers the world's major business regions and gives you access to extensive datasets on companies and decision-makers. Our database is continuously expanding as we add new markets and improve data quality.

## Geographic Coverage

Generect supports lead and company search across most global business markets. Key regions include:

- **North America**: United States, Canada
- **Europe**: United Kingdom, Germany, France, Netherlands, Italy, Spain, Denmark, Norway, Sweden, Finland, Central & Eastern Europe
- **Asia–Pacific**: Singapore, Hong Kong, Japan, South Korea, Australia
- **Other Regions**: Latin America, Middle East, Africa

We support search in a very broad list of countries, and flexible filters help you find the right data in any region.

## Data Types

- **Company Data**: name, industry, size, location, tech stack, website status
- **Contact Data**: full name, job title, corporate email, direct phone number, LinkedIn profile, location

## Data Volume

- Tens of millions of companies
- Hundreds of millions of contacts worldwide
- Continuous updates and coverage expansion
