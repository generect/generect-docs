---
title: 'FAQ for New Users'
description: 'Frequently asked questions about Generect'
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does Generect cost?">
    We operate on a pay-as-you-go model. Searching is free. You only pay $0.03 for each revealed contact and $0.02 for exported leads.  
    The minimum top-up is $20.  
    There are no monthly subscriptions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where does your data come from?">
    We aggregate data from 100+ public and private sources, including LinkedIn, official company websites, public registries, and job boards.  
    Our system updates data in real time to ensure accuracy and freshness.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you guarantee email accuracy?">
    Yes. All emails are verified before being returned.  
    Our deliverability rate is 95%+.  
    If an email turns out to be invalid, we automatically refund the credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does lead search take?">
    Generect works in real time. Most searches take only a few seconds, depending on filters and query complexity.  
    API queries return results almost instantly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Generect integrate with our CRM?">
    Yes. We support native integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, and any custom workflow through our API.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there any search limits?">
    No limits.  
    You can run as many searches as you want – you only pay for revealed or exported contacts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer a trial?">
    Yes. Every new user receives $5 in free credits to test the platform – enough to try lead search, email validation, and phone reveal.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I cannot find the contacts I need?">
    Try adjusting or expanding your filters, using different roles or locations, or searching by a specific LinkedIn profile (company or person) for more precise results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
