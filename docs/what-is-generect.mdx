## What Generect Does

- Finds verified corporate email addresses and direct phone numbers
- Validates email deliverability before sending to reduce bounce rates
- Builds targeted lead lists based on your ICP (industry, company size, location, role, tech stack, and more)
- Enriches existing company and contact data (LinkedIn profiles, domains, industries, technologies, key profile attributes)
- Integrates with your CRM and internal tools via API
- Detects company growth signals (hiring, funding, job openings - available in company profiles)

## Who Generect Is For

- **Founders**: Quickly discover the right potential customers without heavy manual work
- **Lead Generation teams**: Get accurate, verified contacts without extra validation steps
- **Early-stage teams**: Launch outbound with high-quality data from day one
- **Sales managers**: Spend less time cleaning lists and more time closing deals
