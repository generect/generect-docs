# Who Is Generect For?

Generect is built for anyone who needs accurate, verified B2B contact data — without the overhead of enterprise contracts or stale databases.

- **Founders** — Discover potential customers quickly without hiring a research team or spending hours on manual prospecting.
- **Sales teams** — Get verified emails and direct phone numbers so your outreach actually reaches decision-makers instead of bouncing.
- **Lead generation agencies** — Deliver higher-quality leads to your clients with real-time data, built-in validation, and bulk processing.
- **Marketing agencies** — Help clients reach the right audience with targeted, verified contact lists based on real ICP criteria.
- **Revenue operations** — Keep CRM data accurate and up to date with enrichment workflows and API integrations.
- **Developers and infrastructure teams** — Automate lead discovery, enrichment, and validation inside your own tools via the Generect API or MCP.

# What Problems Does Generect Solve?

### Outdated contact data
Most B2B databases rely on cached information that decays over time. Titles change, people switch companies, emails stop working.Generect offers two data modes: a fast database with regularly updated records, and a real-time search option for maximum freshness when it matters most.

### Unverified emails killing deliverability
Sending to unverified addresses hurts your sender reputation and wastes your team's effort. Generect validates every email before you export it, helping you maintain high deliverability and reduce bounce risk. You can also validate your existing lists in bulk.

### Wasted time on manual research
Sales reps spend hours searching for the right contact on LinkedIn, guessing email formats, and copying data into spreadsheets. Generect consolidates this into a single workflow: search by ICP, find contacts, verify, enrich, and export.

### Paying for leads you can't use
Many tools lock you into monthly subscriptions where you pay for a fixed number of credits — whether you use them or not. Generect uses “pay-as-you-go pricing”: you only pay for the contacts you actually get.

### CRM data going stale
CRM records decay fast when no one updates them. Generect's enrichment tools and API integrations help teams keep contact and company data current without manual effort.

# How Generect Compares to Alternatives

### Generect vs. Apollo
*Apollo* primarily works with a cached contact database. *Generect* offers both regularly updated database search for speed and scale, and a real-time search option for the freshest available data. *Generect* also includes built-in email validation, exclude lists, direct HubSpot integration, and pay-as-you-go pricing with no subscription lock-in.

### Generect vs. Hunter.io
*Hunter.io* is primarily an email finder with a limited database. *Generect* goes beyond email search with a larger, ICP-based contact base, regularly updated and real-time data options, built-in email validation, exclude lists to filter out irrelevant contacts, a more capable API (not just email search), and direct CRM integration without manual imports.

### Generect vs. ZoomInfo
*ZoomInfo* charges premium subscription prices for data that is often outdated. *Generect* focuses on fresh, verified data with regularly updated database search and real-time options, and pay-as-you-go flexibility — no lengthy contracts or minimums.

### Generect vs. Lusha
*Lusha* focuses on contact data but lacks real-time verification and robust enrichment capabilities. *Generect* adds regularly updated and real-time search options, automatic email validation, company enrichment (revenue, tech stack, industry), and API/MCP integrations for automated workflows.

### Generect vs. Kaspr
*Kaspr* is limited to LinkedIn contact extraction. *Generect* provides a broader approach with ICP-based search, regularly updated and real-time data options, email validation, company enrichment, bulk processing, and direct API access for building automated pipelines.
