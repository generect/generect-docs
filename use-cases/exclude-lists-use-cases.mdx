## Exclude Existing Customers

Export your CRM customer list and upload it to Exclusions.  
Applying this list ensures your outbound team does not target current customers.

## Hide Leads That Were Already Processed

After completing a search and export:

- Filter results to show only exported leads.
- Add them to an Exclude List.  
  This prevents SDRs from accidentally returning to the same audience.

## Filter Out Competitors or Internal Domains

Create a list of competitors, agencies, or your own company domains.  
Add it to Exclusions to ensure these accounts never appear in your ICP Search results.

## Segment "No-Go" Accounts for Different ICPs

Create separate Exclude Lists for each market or ICP segment, such as:

- "Non-target industries"
- "Low-fit accounts"

Apply them only to the relevant searches to keep results clean and accurate.
