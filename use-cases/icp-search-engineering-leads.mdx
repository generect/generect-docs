## Goal

Find engineering leads in German IT Services companies with 50–200 employees.

## Filters Applied

- Job title: *Software Developer*
- Industry: *IT Services / IT Consulting*
- Location: *Germany*
- Headcount: *50–200*
- Personas: *Engineering*
- Data accuracy period: *30 days*
- Skills: *Software Development, Cloud, DevOps*

## Result

You get a refined list of companies that meet your criteria, plus relevant specialists from those companies who match your ICP by role and skills. This helps you understand the market segment and immediately identify outreach-ready leads.
