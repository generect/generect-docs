## Use Case

Your SDR is searching for VP Sales across multiple regions.  
Instead of setting Job Title + Seniority + Keywords every time, they simply select the saved persona "Sales Leadership."

## How Personas Work

A **persona** is more than a set of job titles – it's a **saved role template** you can use in any ICP Search.

A persona may include:

- **Functions:**  
  Sales, Marketing, Technology, IT, HR, Business Development, Manufacturing, or custom functions.
- **Seniorities:**  
  President, Owner, Founder, VP, C-Level, Head, Director, Manager, Specialist.
- **Keywords (include/exclude):**  
  For fine-tuning profile descriptions or job titles.

**Examples:**

- "Sales Leadership EU"
- "Technical Founders US"
- "HR Directors Global"
